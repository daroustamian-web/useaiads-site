What kind of ad creatives do you generate?
Eight different formats: stat cards, testimonial quotes, before/after transformations, headline CTAs, lifestyle scenes, checklists, social proof cards, and question hooks. AI rotates through them daily so your audience never sees the same ad twice.
How does the AI know my brand?
We scrape your website and analyze your brand voice, colors, services, audience, and differentiators. The AI generates copy that sounds like you — not generic marketing templates. You review everything before it goes live.
Do I need to provide my own creative?
No. That's the whole point. AI generates your ad creatives daily — images and copy — included in every plan. If you have your own photos or videos you'd like to use, we can incorporate those too.
What platforms do you manage?
Facebook and Instagram (Meta). These are where most local and small business ad budgets get the highest return. Google and other platforms available on request.
How much should I spend on ads?
Most of our clients start with $500-$1,500/month in ad spend. Your ad budget goes directly to Meta — we manage it transparently in your own ad account. Our monthly fee covers creative generation and campaign management.
Is there a contract?
No. Month-to-month. If you're not seeing results, cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No penalty, no awkward conversations. We keep clients by performing, not by locking them in.
What results can I expect?
Results vary by industry and budget. Our healthcare clients have generated $2.9M in additional annual revenue. Our lead gen clients average $14-$18 per qualified lead. We'll give you honest projections on the strategy call based on your specific business.
