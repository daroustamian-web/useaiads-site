How does AI generate ads for my agency?
We analyze your website, services, service area, and brand voice. AI generates multiple ad creatives daily — images and copy — that rotate through proven formats: testimonial quotes, caregiver-focused messaging, family decision-maker targeting, and more. You review everything before it goes live.
What platforms do you run ads on?
Facebook and Instagram (Meta). This is where families and adult children of seniors spend time and make care decisions. Google Ads available on request for agencies targeting high-intent search traffic.
How much should I spend on ads?
Most of our in-home care clients start with $500–$1,500/month in ad spend. Your ad budget goes directly to Meta — we manage it transparently in your own ad account. Our monthly fee covers creative generation and campaign management.

All lead gen metrics come directly from Meta Ads Manager — 318 leads for Brightwood Health in Arizona, 336 leads for Fresh Start Healthcare in New Jersey. Our CBAS center revenue numbers come from California's state-published monthly attendance reports.
Is there a contract?
No. Month-to-month. If you're not seeing results, cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We keep clients by performing, not by locking them in.
How quickly will I see leads?
Most in-home care agencies see their first leads within the first week. Consistent lead flow typically starts within 2–3 weeks as campaigns optimize and best-performing ads get scaled.
Can you help me recruit caregivers too?
Yes. Many of our clients run both client acquisition and caregiver recruitment campaigns. We can target CNAs, HHAs, and caregivers in your area with job-focused ad creatives while simultaneously running client-facing campaigns.
